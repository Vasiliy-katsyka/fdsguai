LobsterCoin FAQ

How to Play

Tap the lobster as fast as you can to earn clicks! Clicks are the main currency in LobsterCoin. Use them to buy upgrades, mine crypto, purchase skins, and more!

How to Get More Clicks

There are multiple ways to boost your click earnings:

  • Click Multiplier: Upgrade your click multiplier in the "Boosts" menu to earn more clicks per tap. This is a great early game investment.
  • Mining Cards: Purchase mining cards in the "Mining" menu to passively generate clicks over time. Each card represents a different investment opportunity.
  • Daily Tasks: Complete daily tasks in the "Earn" menu to earn bonus clicks. These tasks can include watching ads, subscribing to social media channels, and more.
  • Referral Program: Share your referral link with friends to earn even more clicks. The more players you refer, the bigger the bonus!
  • Skins: Some skins provide a boost to your profit per hour. Try collecting different skins to maximize your earnings.
  • Daily Rewards: Claim your daily reward in the "Earn" menu for a substantial bonus of clicks. The reward increases each day, following the Fibonacci sequence.
  • Watch Ads: Watch ads to earn extra clicks. You can earn more clicks by watching a set amount of ads.

What are Mining Cards?

Mining cards represent investments in different cryptocurrencies and assets. Each card has a different price and generates clicks per hour based on its level. You can upgrade your mining cards to increase their profitability.

What are Skins?

Skins are purely cosmetic, but they can also offer boosts to your profit per hour. Collect different skins to personalize your lobster and improve your earnings!

How Do I Apply Promo Codes?

Go to the "Settings" menu and enter a promo code in the provided field. Promo codes can grant you free clicks, unlock special bonuses, or give you access to exclusive content. You can get promo code on our telegram channel or from your friends.

What is the Click Limit?

You can only tap the lobster a certain number of times per minute. This limit exists to prevent abuse and maintain a fair gameplay experience. You can upgrade your click limit in the "Boosts" menu to increase the number of clicks you can earn per minute.

What Can I Do in the "Settings" Menu?

The "Settings" menu offers a variety of customization and management options:

  • Language: Change the language of the game to your preferred language.
  • Clear Data: Reset your game progress and start fresh. This will remove all your earned clicks, purchased upgrades, and other data.
  • Legal Info: View the game's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  • Theme: Customize the game's appearance by changing colors, backgrounds, and other visual elements.
  • Apply Promo Codes: Enter promo codes to unlock bonuses and benefits.

What is the "Airdrop"?

The "Airdrop" is a special event where LobsterCoin will distribute $LOB tokens to players based on their game progress. The amount of tokens you receive will be determined by your balance, league, profit per hour, ads watched, and purchased skins. Stay tuned for details on the Airdrop!
